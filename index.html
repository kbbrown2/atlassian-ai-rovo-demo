What is the Atlassian AI Ecosystem?

This section introduces the foundational concepts of Atlassian's AI strategy. It clarifies the relationship between **Atlassian Intelligence**, the underlying AI engine, and **Rovo AI**, the user-facing suite of tools. Understanding this distinction is crucial for grasping how AI capabilities are integrated and delivered across Atlassian products to enhance team performance.

Rovo AI

The Advanced AI Teammate

Think of Rovo as the interactive application layer of Atlassian's AI. It's a powerful suite of tools designed to unify your tools, knowledge, and teams into a single, intelligent experience. It's what you directly interact with to get work done faster.

  • ➤
    Purpose: Enhance team performance, automate tasks, and provide instant access to information.
  • ➤
    Key Components: AI Agents, Rovo Chat, Rovo Studio, and powerful cross-app search.

Atlassian Intelligence

The Foundational AI Engine

This is the core platform that powers Rovo and all other AI features across Atlassian products. It works behind the scenes, leveraging proprietary models, OpenAI technology, and the unique "Teamwork Graph" to understand your organization's specific context.

  • ➤
    Technology: A combination of proprietary models, OpenAI, and your unique Teamwork Graph.
  • ➤
    Focus: Boost productivity, leverage organizational data, and enable data-driven decisions.

Deep Dive: Rovo AI

This section provides a dedicated exploration of Rovo AI, Atlassian's advanced AI platform designed to be your intelligent teammate. It details Rovo's key components and how they work together to unify tools, knowledge, and teams, driving efficiency and informed decision-making across your organization.

Rovo's Core Components

  • ●
    AI Agents: These are tailored AI models designed to perform specific tasks. Rovo comes with over 20 ready-to-use agents (e.g., Issue Organizer, Service Triage Assistant) and allows for the creation of custom agents through Rovo Studio to automate complex workflows, gather information, and improve efficiency.
  • ●
    Rovo Chat: An intelligent conversational agent, similar to a GenAI chatbot, integrated into Atlassian products like Confluence. It allows users to ask natural language questions and receive contextual answers, summarize content, and even trigger actions like creating Jira issues directly from the chat interface.
  • ●
    Rovo Studio: A powerful, low-code/no-code builder that empowers teams (even non-technical users) to create custom AI agents and automations. It's a unified experience for building tailored AI logic and integrating it with existing workflows.
  • ●
    Advanced Search & Knowledge Cards: Rovo leverages Atlassian Intelligence to provide AI-powered search capabilities across your entire Atlassian ecosystem and connected third-party apps (e.g., Slack, Google Drive). Knowledge Cards provide instant, contextual definitions and insights within your workflow.
  • ●
    Teamwork Graph: The underlying engine that powers Rovo's personalization. It connects people, work, and knowledge across your organization to deliver highly relevant and accurate AI insights.

Why Rovo Matters

Rovo AI is designed to address common workplace challenges by:

  • ➤
    **Reducing Context Switching:** By unifying tools and knowledge, Rovo minimizes the need to jump between different applications.
  • ➤
    **Automating Repetitive Tasks:** AI Agents take over mundane work, freeing up teams to focus on higher-value activities.
  • ➤
    **Accelerating Decision-Making:** Instant access to relevant information and AI-powered insights enables faster, more informed choices.
  • ➤
    **Improving Collaboration:** By keeping everyone on the same page and providing shared intelligence, Rovo fosters more cohesive teamwork.

Core AI Capabilities

This section highlights the universal AI capabilities that Atlassian Intelligence brings to its entire product suite. These features are designed to provide a consistent and powerful intelligent experience, regardless of which Atlassian application you are using. Each card represents a key area where AI enhances productivity and workflow.

Natural Language Interaction

Ask questions and search for work using everyday language. The AI translates your intent into complex queries (like JQL) automatically.

Content Generation & Enhancement

Draft, edit, translate, and reformat content on the fly. Improve your writing and adjust the tone with a single click.

Intelligent Summaries

Get concise summaries of long pages, comment threads, and whiteboards to catch up instantly without the reading.

Automated Workflows

Create complex automation rules using simple prompts. The AI can also suggest and create tasks based on context from pages or chats.

Contextual Definitions

Instantly get definitions for company-specific jargon, project codenames, and acronyms directly within your workflow.

Cross-Tool Knowledge

Rovo's search and agents pull information not just from Atlassian tools, but also from connected apps like Slack, Google Drive, and GitHub.
